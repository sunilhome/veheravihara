This temple, located in the village of Gangodawila in the Colombo District of the Western Province, has a history of about six hundred years. The Gangodawila Sri Vijayarama Rajamaha Viharaya, built during the reign of King Sri Parakramabahu VI, was founded by the Most Venerable Asiyadeniye Dhammakitthi Maha Swamin. After the arrival of the Portuguese, this temple fell into decline. It is currently represented by the current incumbent, Saddharma Keerthi Sri Amunumulla Jinarathana Swamin. Furthermore, this temple is also the headquarters of the Vijayarama Sangha Parapura belonging to the Galgoda Sri Viharaya Parshwa of the Kalyani Samagri Dharma Maha Sangha Sabha in Sri Jayawardenapura Kotte. The historical Buddha temple, Bodhi tree, Chaitya Raja Thero and the relic house established in this temple, which is developing day by day due to the great dedication of both the incumbent and the lay and ordained, are very beautiful in this temple.
Among the main monks who have maintained this valuable temple, Venerable Thalawathugoda Sri Jinarathana Nayaka Thero and Dampe Sri Rathanasara Nayaka Thero are the main ones.
The Gangodawila Sri Vijayarama Rajamaha Viharaya, which is developing day by day due to the dedication, effort and courage of the venerable Sangha including the current incumbent, Saddharma Keerthi Sri Venerable Amunumullae Jinarathana Thero, is an attractive shrine in the Kotte city of Sri Jayawardenapura.
This temple, located within the historic Kotte Kingdom, has been declared a sacred site since September 2023.
