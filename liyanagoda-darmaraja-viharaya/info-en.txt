Liyanagoda Dharmaraja Viharaya is an ancient temple located in Liyanagoda village in the Colombo District of the Western Province. The founding head monk was Tangalle Gnanalankara Thero. After that, his disciple Venerable Medagama Gnaniswara Nayaka Thero maintained the temple. During that period, due to the dedication and enthusiasm of the Thero, this temple, which is about a hundred years old, was established as a complete building. After the Thero passed away on 24 May 2025, the Rajakaya Pandita Sastravade Kotte Rahula Thero has been serving as the incumbent of the temple.
One of the main old Dhamma schools in this area, about three hundred and fifty children receive Dhamma knowledge here. A milk meal is provided to everyone in the morning. This temple belongs to the Kalyanawansa Chapter of the Amarapura Nikaya. Shasanarakshaka Bala Mandala, Pannipitiya.
