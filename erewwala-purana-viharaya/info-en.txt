The ancient Raja Maha Viharaya of Arewwala, located in the village of Arewwala in the Colombo District of the Western Province, is about two hundred years old. The founding incumbent was Venerable Wakwellae Punyasiri Thero. The current incumbent, Venerable Kotapola Mahinda Thero, has been the incumbent of this temple for about fifty years. The Thero has served as the Deputy Principal of Vidyaloka Vidyalaya for twenty-five years. He also serves as the Chairman of the Kesbewa Sasanarakshaka Samiti in Arewwala. He runs a Dhamma school for the children of the area. Currently, more than five hundred children are studying there.
Thero, who is known by the titles of Honorary Shastravade Shastrapati Pandita, Chief Sanghanayake of the Western Province and Galle District, is also a Justice of the Peace of Sri Lanka and the Chairman of the Kesbewa Sasanarakshaka Samiti.
