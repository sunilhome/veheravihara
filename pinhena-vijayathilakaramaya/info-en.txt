The Sri Wijayathilakarama Viharaya in Pinhena, Kottawa is a temple that is about one hundred and thirty-eight years old.
The founding incumbent was Mahalwarawa Gnanathilakabidhana Nayaka Mahimi Thero, who held the Anunayaka Dura of the Sri Kalyani Samagri Dharma Maha Sangha Sabha in Kotte. He had five disciples.
The last of them was Pallemulla Nandaloka Thero, who succeeded Mahalwarawa Mahinda Nayaka Thero as the Incumbent of Sri Wijayathilakarama Viharaya. After his death,
their senior disciple, Sarada Thero, a graduate student at the University of Jayewardenepura, became the Incumbent of Sri Wijayathilakarama Viharaya in Pinhena.
Currently, the Sri Vijayathilakarama Temple, as an ancient historical shrine in this area, contains all the temple accessories that should be included in a temple.
This temple, which has a relic house, a temple house, a Bodhirajayanan, a Chaityaajayanan, three Sanghawasa Seema Malakaya, a  Dharmashala,
and an almshouse that is under construction, is a very valuable sacred shrine in the Colombo District of the Western Province.
