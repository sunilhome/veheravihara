This Parama Dhamma Viharaya is located in Pannipitiya village, Colombo District, Western Province.
The construction of a permanent monastery at the temple began in 1921 and the construction of the bell tower was carried out in 1927. Venerable Athurugiriya Saddhatissa and Venerable Aravwala Sumanatissa were enthroned in 1921. The construction of the temple building began in 1924.
The Dharmashala was opened in 1927. The foundation stone of the pagoda was laid in 1950.

The Parama Dhamma Dhamma School was inaugurated in 1952 and the Nidhannotsava of the pagoda was held in 1953. The unveiling of the Chaitya Kotha took place in 1955. The repose of Venerable Kottawe Premarathana Thero took place in 1956 and the venerable Professor Pannipitiya Piyadassi passed away in 1960. The inauguration of the Sri Piyadarshi Library and Dhamma School building took place in 1969. The Venerable Saddhatissa Maha Thero of Athurugiriya passed away in 1980. Venerable Dehikumbure Bodhisiri Thero took place in 1977. In 1984, the foundation stone of the three-storey Sanghavasa with the relic hall and the alms hall was laid. The foundation stone of the Bodhi wall with twenty-eight Buddha statues and a canopy was laid in 1998 and the Bodhi wall was inaugurated in 1999. The new Sanghavasa was built in 2001.
