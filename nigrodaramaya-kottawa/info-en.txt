This Nigrodharamaya is located near the village of Kottawa in the Colombo District of the Western Province. Two hundred years ago, a non-sectarian ascetic who ordained himself as a monk lived there. Since 1986, after the death of the last ascetic who lived there,
this temple has been managed by a board of trustees. The current incumbent, who was in office about thirty years ago, has dedicated himself to ensuring that all the work here is done properly.
In addition to the Asathu Bodhi, there is also an ancient Nuga Bodhi. There is an ancient lamp post (lamp house) and in the past, the light obtained at night was the light of the coconut oil lamps lit in that lamp house. The old paintings here belong to the Kotte period.
There is a Dhamma school called Sandavimana in this temple. About four hundred Buddhist children are currently receiving Dhamma knowledge there. A very valuable ancient temple located in the Western Province.
