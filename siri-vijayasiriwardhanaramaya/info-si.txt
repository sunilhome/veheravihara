Although this Siriwardhanaramaya Viharaya was founded in 1829, it was restored as a shrine in 1889 under the leadership of the Premarathana Loku Hamuduruwan of Thalapathpitiya.
The current incumbent of the temple is Venerable Senapura Nandarathana Thero.
An ancient temple, there is a Sirivijayasiri Sahana
Foundation. Welfare activities are carried out from it, especially the foundation that provides assistance to children who are studying to improve their education. In addition, after the death of a member, an amount of sixty thousand rupees is donated to the surviving family. In addition, all the necessary huts, chairs, garden lids and tools are provided free of charge.
The local voluntary organizations operate based on the temple. A Dhamma school is run for the children of the area in this temple.
This temple provides a great service to the area in religious and social terms.
