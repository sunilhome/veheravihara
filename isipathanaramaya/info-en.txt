The founding incumbent of Nawinna Isipathanaramaya is Venerable Hokandara Dhammananda Mahimin Thero.
He purchased the land from his uncle in 1906 and started this temple. Since then, this sacred land has remained a private property until 2022.
Since then, Nawinna Isipathanaramaya has been restored as a temple affiliated to the Maha Viharaya of Valukaramaya in Kollupitiya as a tribute to Pandita Walane Siddhartha Maha Nayaka Thero.
The new Devala premises were opened in 2014 under the guidance of the Most Venerable Maharagama Nanda Nayaka Thero and the Darshanapati of Isipathanarama, Venerable Nahalle Vajaragnana Thero.
