The Siri Vijaya Nandanarama Viharaya is located in the Egodawatta village of Arawala in the Colombo District of the Western Province. The current incumbent is the Venerable Kalawane Rathanasara Thero. He also serves as a teacher at Lumbini Vidyalaya. It has been about one hundred and eighty years since this Viharaya was founded. The founding chief incumbent was Venerable Hemarathana Thero of Thalapath Pitiye. After him, the Venerable Somarama Thero of Thalangama and then the Venerable Piyarathana Thero of Handugala have maintained this Viharaya. The current incumbent came to this Viharaya in 2001 but was ordained on 29th May 2006. He has now spent twenty years as a monk and was ordained in 2013. After the death of Venerable Handugala Piyarathana, the Thero is acting as the incumbent of the temple.
A Dhamma school has been operating here for a long time. In the early days, more than four hundred students came here to gain Dhamma knowledge. After the Covid disaster, about one hundred and fifty children currently come to gain Dhamma knowledge. The current Venerable Thero is the headmaster of the Dhamma
school.
Poya Dina Sil Samathan programs are held here. Five sermons and Bodhi Pooja are held on that day.
Bodhi Pooja is held every Saturday evening. It is a Buddhist temple that does a great service to the area.
