This temple is located on the Mahamegarama Road, about half a kilometer from Maharagama in the Colombo District of the Western Province. The founding incumbent of this temple, founded in 1953, was Ankumbure Sugunapala Nayaka Thero. After his passing, Venerable Ankumbure Amaravansa Thero has been the incumbent since 1992. The Sri Sugunapala Dhamma School, registered in 1990, was started here. It has been continuously maintained to this day. It is dedicated to the Dhamma education of Buddhist children in the area. About four hundred children are currently studying in this Buddhist institution. Among the special Buddhist programs held every Poya Day, many offerings including Dhamma sermons, Bodhi Pooja, Pahan Pooja, etc. are performed. This Buddhist shrine has become popular with all Buddhists living in the area.
