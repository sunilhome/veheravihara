This Nalandarama Temple, located near Maharagama in the Colombo District of the Western Province, was started in 1954. The land for the construction of this temple was donated by Mr. Embul Deniye David Signo Upasaka. At that time, the founding incumbent was Madiwela Rathanasara Nayaka Thero. After that, Udahamulla Saranapala Thero maintained the temple. Since then,
Subhadrarama Pirivena's Dr. Thalapathpitiya Pungnaloka Nayaka Thero has been the incumbent of this sacred site. This temple has a very valuable and beautiful relic house.
The foundation stone of the two-storey Sanghavasa with a library was laid in 2003.
Its work was completed and it was inaugurated in 2008 after a Pirithran Dhamma sermon.
The Sri Rathanapala Dhamma School operates to improve the Dhamma knowledge of children in the area.
