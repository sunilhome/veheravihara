This Nalandarama Temple, located near Maharagama in the Colombo District of the Western Province, was started in 1954. The land for the construction of this temple was donated by Mr. Embul Deniye David Signo Upasaka. At that time, the founding incumbent was Madiwela Rathanasara Nayaka Thero. After that, Udahamulla Saranapala Thero maintained the temple. Since then,
Subhadrarama Pirivena's Dr. Thalapathpitiya Pungnaloka Nayaka Thero has been the incumbent of this sacred site. This temple has a very valuable and beautiful relic hall.
The foundation stone of this two-storey Sanghavasa with a library was laid in 2003.
The work was completed and inaugurated in 2008 after a thirty-seven-hour pirithran Dhamma sermon with door-to-door seating.
The Sri Rathanapala Dhamma School is operating to develop the Dhamma knowledge of children in the area.
